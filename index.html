Niraj Gupta Impex Pvt. LTD

NIRAJ GUPTA IMPEX PVT. LTD. has been an internationally acknowledged indenting and sourcing agent in India since 2005. We have expanded our operations to include Importers, Merchant Exporters, and Job Manufacturing. Our company has been associated with the manufacturing of dyes and dye intermediates since 1993.

Our primary activities include international trade promotion for a wide range of industries including paints, coatings, resins, specialty chemicals, organic & inorganic dyes, pharmaceuticals, bulk drugs & API, construction & water treatment, electronics, plastic & polymers, rubber & textiles, and many more.
